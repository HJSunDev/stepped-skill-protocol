# Expected Handoff Sequence

## After `steps/collect.md`

```markdown
## SSP Handoff

- Step: collect
- Output: source map, key facts, caveats, and unresolved questions
- Carry Forward:
  - topic and scope
  - strongest sources
  - key facts
  - caveats and uncertainty
  - unresolved questions
  - source constraints
- Ready For Next: yes
```

## After `steps/synthesize.md`

```markdown
## SSP Handoff

- Step: synthesize
- Output: synthesized findings and recommended brief structure
- Carry Forward:
  - major findings
  - evidence strength
  - caveats and uncertainty
  - recommended brief structure
  - final-answer constraints
- Ready For Next: yes
```

## After `steps/finalize.md`

No handoff is required. The final step produces the user-facing answer and terminates at `END`.
