# Step: Synthesize Findings

## Objective

Turn the collected material into a structured set of findings. This step's deliverable is synthesis, not final prose.

## Resources

None

## Instructions

1. Use the handoff from the collection step as inherited state.
2. Group facts into 3-5 major findings.
3. Separate strong evidence, weak evidence, and uncertainty.
4. Identify contradictions, missing evidence, and claims that should be softened.
5. Do not write the final user-facing brief yet.

## Output

Produce:

- synthesized findings;
- evidence strength by finding;
- caveats and uncertainty;
- recommended brief structure;
- final-answer constraints.

## Completion Criteria

This step is complete when the finalization step can write the brief without revisiting raw collection.

## Handoff

Carry forward:

- major findings;
- evidence strength;
- caveats and uncertainty;
- recommended brief structure;
- final-answer constraints.

Before reading `Next`, record an `SSP Handoff` block with those items in the execution context.
