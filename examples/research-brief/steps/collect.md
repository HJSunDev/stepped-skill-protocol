# Step: Collect Material

## Objective

Collect the material needed for the research brief. This step's deliverable is source material, not final prose.

## Resources

- `references/source-guidelines.md`

## Instructions

1. Identify the user's topic, scope, desired depth, and any source constraints.
2. Use the listed resources to guide source selection and note quality.
3. Build a source map with key facts, source notes, caveats, and unresolved questions.
4. Keep interpretation minimal. Do not write the final brief in this step.

## Output

Produce:

- topic and scope statement;
- source map;
- key facts;
- caveats and uncertainty;
- unresolved questions.

## Completion Criteria

This step is complete when the synthesis step can evaluate the material without redoing source collection.

## Handoff

Carry forward:

- topic and scope;
- strongest sources;
- key facts;
- caveats and uncertainty;
- unresolved questions;
- any source constraints that must affect the final brief.

Before reading `Next`, record an `SSP Handoff` block with those items in the execution context.
