# Severity Scale

Use this scale to prioritize review findings.

## Critical

The artifact is likely to fail its main purpose or create serious harm unless fixed.

## High

The artifact has a substantial flaw that will likely cause poor outcomes, blocked adoption, or expensive rework.

## Medium

The artifact has a meaningful weakness, but there is a clear path to correct it without redesigning the whole work.

## Low

The artifact has a small issue, polish gap, or localized ambiguity.

## Informational

The point is useful context but not a defect.

Confidence:

- high: directly supported by evidence;
- medium: supported by evidence plus reasonable inference;
- low: plausible, but evidence is incomplete.
