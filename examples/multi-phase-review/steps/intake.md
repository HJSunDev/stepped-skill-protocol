# Step: Intake

## Objective

Identify what is being reviewed, why it matters, and what standard the review should apply.

## Resources

- `references/review-rubric.md`

## Instructions

1. Identify the review target and its artifact type: design, plan, document, implementation, product concept, or release candidate.
2. Identify the intended audience and decision context.
3. Identify explicit review criteria from the user.
4. If criteria are missing, use the review rubric as the default.
5. Do not inspect details deeply or make recommendations yet.

## Output

Produce:

- review target summary;
- audience and decision context;
- review criteria;
- missing information or assumptions;
- review plan.

## Completion Criteria

This step is complete when the inspection step has enough context to collect relevant evidence.

## Handoff

Carry forward:

- target summary;
- audience;
- decision context;
- review criteria;
- assumptions and missing information;
- review plan.

Before reading `Next`, record an `SSP Handoff` block with those items in the execution context.
