# Step: Inspect Evidence

## Objective

Inspect the target and collect evidence. This step's deliverable is evidence, not judgment or recommendations.

## Resources

- `references/review-rubric.md`

## Instructions

1. Use the intake handoff as inherited state.
2. Inspect the target against the selected criteria.
3. Capture evidence with location, quote or summary, and relevance.
4. Separate direct evidence from inference.
5. Note missing evidence, contradictions, and questions.
6. Do not prioritize or recommend fixes yet.

## Output

Produce:

- evidence map;
- direct observations;
- inferred risks;
- missing evidence;
- questions for evaluation.

## Completion Criteria

This step is complete when the evaluation step can judge quality without re-inspecting the whole target.

## Handoff

Carry forward:

- evidence map;
- direct observations;
- inferred risks;
- missing evidence;
- unresolved questions;
- criteria used during inspection.

Before reading `Next`, record an `SSP Handoff` block with those items in the execution context.
